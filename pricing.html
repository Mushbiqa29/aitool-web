Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. When you cancel, you'll continue to have access to the paid features until the end of your current billing period.

Do I need a subscription to submit tools?

Yes, tool submission is a feature available to Pro and Business plan subscribers. This helps us maintain a high-quality directory by ensuring submissions come from committed members of our community.

What's included in the Business plan?

The Business plan includes all Pro features plus: featured listings for your tools, team access for up to 5 members, advanced analytics about how users interact with your listings, and 24/7 priority support. It's ideal for companies and agencies that want to maximize their visibility in our directory.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for both Pro and Business plans. You can explore all the premium features before committing to a paid subscription. No credit card is required to start the trial.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team at sales@ainexus.com with details about your organization to learn more about our discount programs.
